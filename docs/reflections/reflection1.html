Reflection 1

Baseline Self-Assessment

Establishing your starting point

Overview

This reflection is designed to establish a baseline for your knowledge and skills at the beginning of the semester. There are no right or wrong answers—we want to understand where you're starting from so you (and we) can measure your growth throughout the course.

Write quickly and honestly. Get all of your ideas out, even if they're vague impressions or associations. You won't be graded on the quality of this writing—this is purely for establishing your baseline.

Reflection Prompt

Please write approximately 250 words addressing the following questions:

Digital Culture Proficiency

Off the top of your head, based on the knowledge you bring with you to the course and your experience as a DCDA major or minor:

  • How proficient do you feel in your skills and content knowledge regarding "Digital Culture"?
  • Give an example of what you're comfortable with and/or what you find challenging about your abilities here.

Data Analytics Proficiency

Similarly, reflect on your data analytics background:

  • How proficient do you feel in your skills and content knowledge regarding "Data Analytics"?
  • Give an example of what you're comfortable with and/or what you find challenging about your abilities here.

Looking Ahead

Additionally, please address:

  • What are you looking forward to regarding the capstone course?
  • What are you concerned about (or even dreading) regarding the capstone course?
Remember: Write quickly, trying to get all of your ideas out—even if they are just vague impressions or associations. There is no right or wrong answer to these questions, and you won't be graded on this writing. This assignment is designed to establish a baseline that you can use later in the course to measure your learning.

Submission

Submit your reflection (approximately 250 words) to D2L.

  • You may submit as a text entry or attach a document (Word, PDF, etc.)
  • This is not graded for quality—only for completion
  • Be honest and thorough in your self-assessment
