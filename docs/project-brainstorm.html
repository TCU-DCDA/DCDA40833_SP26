AI Brainstorming Activity

Using AI to Develop Your Research Question

Overview

This ungraded activity guides you through a structured conversation with ChatGPT or Claude to help develop your Semester Project research question. You may complete this individually or in a group.

Not submitted. Record your reflections in your own notes (or your group's shared notes). These are for your benefit as you develop your proposal — you don't need to turn them in.
Citation reminder. If you draw on the AI conversation in your Project Proposal, include relevant excerpts and cite the tool appropriately. Do not present AI-generated text as your own thinking without attribution.

Before You Begin

Decide which project path you're pursuing before you open the AI tool. If you're working in a group, align on this first — the path shapes everything that follows.

Option 1: Data Analysis (DA)

A Python-driven data analysis and visualization project on a topic of your choosing.

Option 2: Digital Culture (DC)

A web development or multimodal authoring project focused on a research topic or creative endeavor engaging digital culture. Does not require strict data analysis.

When you make your first request to ChatGPT or Claude, include:

  • Your chosen path (Option 1: DA or Option 2: DC)
  • Any topic interests you're already leaning toward
  • The 7-week project timeline

Step 1: Generate Research Questions

Use ChatGPT or Claude to generate 10 research questions. Specify your project path, any topic interests, and that you have 7 weeks to complete the project.

In your notes: How useful are the results? What possible problems or shortcomings are there in what was generated?

Step 2: Narrow the Field

Double back and ask ChatGPT or Claude which questions it thinks are most viable for a 7-week project.

In your notes: How useful are the results? What possible problems or shortcomings are there in what was generated?

Step 3: Explore Methodology

Referencing the question you like most, ask what research methodology you would need for a 7-week project based on that question.

In your notes: How useful are the results? What possible problems or shortcomings are there in what was generated?

Step 4: Identify Limitations

Follow up by asking what are some possible problems with or limitations to your research question for a 7-week project.

In your notes: How useful are the results? What possible problems or shortcomings are there in what was generated?

Step 5: Overall Reflection

In general, what are your thoughts about using AI as a brainstorming tool in this context?

  • Overall, how useful did you find its replies to your prompts?
  • How much of the content generated do you plan to use as you move forward?
  • What would you do differently in a future AI-assisted brainstorming session?

Using Your Results

The goal of this activity is to arrive at a stronger, more refined research question — and to get a preliminary sense of the methodology and scope your project will require. Carry what's useful into your Project Proposal.

If you include AI-generated content in your proposal

Paste relevant excerpts from the conversation and cite the tool. Do not present AI-generated text as your own thinking without attribution.
